Privacy Policy

RaySource UK · Last updated: 26 March 2026

TL;DR:

  • We don't require an account, and we don't collect your name, real email address, or any login credentials.
  • We temporarily log your IP address for security and abuse preventions; it's deleted after 1 hour unless you've violated our Terms, in which case it may be retained as a permanent ban.
  • For BurnerMail, emails received by your temporary address are stored only to display them to you and are automatically deleted after 1 hour.
  • We don't use cookies, serve ads, run third-party analytics, or sell your data to anyone.
  • Our services are hosted on Cloudflare.
  • UK and EEA users have standard data rights under UK GDPR, contact us to exercise them.

RaySource UK ("we", "us", "our") are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our Services, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. We encourage you to read this policy carefully alongside our Terms of Service.

1. Who We Are

RaySource, also known under the name RaySource UK, is a personal project operated by Alex O'Sullivan in the United Kingdom. This service is provided for free and does not operate as a commercial business. Our Services are provided via raysource.co.uk.

2. Our Privacy Philosophy

Our Services are designed from the ground up in mind. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, or supply a real email address. We collect as little information as is necessary to operate our Services, and we do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 IP Addresses

When you use our Services, your IP address is automatically processed by our infrastructure and deleted after 1 hour (60 minutes). We collect and store your IP address for the following purposes:

  • Rate limiting: To prevent abuse of our Services by limiting the number of requests any single user can make within a given time window.
  • Abuse prevention: To detect and block automated bots, scripted attacks, and other misuse of our Services.
  • IP banning: Where a user has violated our Terms of Service, their IP address may be stored with a "banned" flag. These bans are permanent by default to ensure the ongoing security of our infrastructure.
  • IP-to-Email: To enforce our BurnerMail Banned Senders policy, we maintain a temporary link between your IP address and the specific temporary email address you are currently accessing.

    • Rate-limiting records are stored for a maximum of 1 minute (60 seconds). Ban records expire after 1 day (24 hours) unless a longer restriction is applied manually following a serious or repeated violation.

3.2 BurnerMail: Email Data

When an email is received by your BurnerMail temporary email address, we store the following information temporarily in order to display it to you:

  • The sender's email address.
  • The subject line.
  • The raw body of the email.
  • A timestamp indicating when the email was received.

All email data is automatically and permanently deleted after 1 hour (60 minutes) from the time of receipt. We do not read, analyse, or share the contents of emails for any purpose other than displaying them to you.

3.3 BurnerMail: Filtering & Blacklisting

To protect our BurnerMail service and Users, we maintain the following restricted lists:

  • Restricted Senders: We maintain a list of known malicious sender addresses. Emails from these addresses are rejected immediately and never stored in our systems.
  • Banned Senders: We maintain a list of prohibited sender domains and specific email address (e.g., streaming services or prohibited content).

If a User attempts to receive an email from a source of the Banned Senders list, this transaction is flagged. Upon the User's attempt to access the inbox for that specific address, a permanent IP ban is issued to protect our infrastructure and ensure compliance with our BurnerMail Guidelines.

3.4 BurnerMail: Abuse Reports

If you submit a report of abusive content via our reporting feature, we store the email identifier, the reason you provided, and your IP address. This information is retained solely for the purpose of reviewing the reported content and taking appropriate action.

To protect the privacy of all parties, abuse reports are automatically purged from our systems 2 days (48 hours) after submission. We aim to review all reports within this window. However, as this is a privacy-first automated process, reports submitted immediately before or during weekends may be deleted before a manual review can take place. For urgent matters requiring immediate attention please email: urgent@raysource.co.uk.

3.5 What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, real email address, or any account credentials.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertisement purposes.
  • We do not use third-party analytics services (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • We do not serve advertisements.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

For Users in the United Kingdom (UK) and European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your IP address is in our legitimate interest in protecting the integrity and security of our Services, and in preventing abuse. This processing is proportionate to the risks involved and does not override your rights and freedoms.

5. Data Retention

The following retention periods apply to data we process:

  • Rate-limiting records: Deleted automatically after 1 minute (60 seconds).
  • IP Address: Deleted automatically after 1 hour (60 minutes), unless the IP is flagged for a violation, in which case it is retain as an IP ban record.
  • BurnerMail: Temporary email data: Deleted automatically after 1 hour (60 minutes) from the time of receipt.
  • BurnerMail: IP-to-Email: Deleted automatically after 1 hour (60 minutes), tied to the lifespan of temporary email data, unless a violation triggers a permanent IP ban.
  • BurnerMail: Abuse reports: Deleted automatically after 2 days (48 hours) to balance privacy with administrative review.
  • IP ban records: Retained indefinitely to ensure the ongoing security and integrity of our Service. These records are only reviewed and removed upon a successful manual appeal.
  • Support Correspondence: Emails and support tickets are manually deleted once the matter is resolved and the information is no longer required, with a maximum retention period of 30 days.

For BurnerMail Abuse reports: Please note that because this process is automated, reports submitted over weekends may be purged before manual review can occur. Please read Section 3.4 for a more detailed explanation.

To appeal an IP ban, please get in contact.

6. Data Sharing, Third Parties & International Data Transfers

6.1 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except in the following limited circumstance:

  • Legal obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, or court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of RaySource UK, our Users, or others.

6.2 Third Parties (Infrastructure Providers)

To provide a secure and reliable experience, we use third-party infrastructure providers. These partners only process data as necessary to preform their specific functions for RaySource:

  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for content delivery and security. They process technical data (such as IP addresses) to protect against attacks and speed up site delivery. Their processing is governed by the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub: Our Site is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub collects basic visitor logs to maintain service stability and security, as outlined in the GitHub Privacy Statement. We use this service to keep RaySource free and ad-free.
  • Zoho: We use Zoho Mail for our support system. Any information sent us via email is stored and processed by Zoho to help us address your inquiries. Use of this service is subject to the Zoho UK Privacy Policy. To protect your privacy, all correspondence related to a support ticket is manually deleted once the matter is resolved and the information is no longer required, with a maximum retention period of 30 days, as explained in Section 5.

6.3 International Data Transfer

Because we use global providers like Cloudflare, GitHub, and Zoho, your information may be processed in countries outside the UK or EEA, primarily the United States. Since these countries may have different data protection laws, we ensure your data remains protected using the following legal safeguards:

  • Data Privacy Framework (UK-US Data Bridge): We use this for certified partners like Cloudflare and GitHub.
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): For partners not covered by the framework, such as Zoho, we use specific legal contracts approved by the UK and EU to ensure your data receives the same level of protection it has at home.
  • Adequacy Decisions: We only transfer data to countries that the UK government has officially recognised as having high data protection standards.

7. Your Rights

If you are located in the United Kingdom (UK) or the European Economic Area (EEA) you have the following rights in relation to the personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of erasure: You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligation we have to retain it.
  • Right of object: You have the right to object to our processing of your data on the basis of legitimate interests.
  • Right of restriction: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Please note that because we collect very limited personal data and most of it expires automatically within minutes or hours, it may not be possible to identify or retrieve your specific records by the time a request is received. To exercise any of these rights, please get in touch.

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our Services are delivered over HTTPS, and data is stored using Cloudflare's Key-Value infrastructure with strict access controls.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please get in touch and we will take steps to delete it.

Please note, as explained in Section 7, that because we collect very limited personal data and most of it expires automatically within minutes or hours, it may not be possible to identify or retrieve specific records by the time a request is received.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

As we do not require you to create an account or provide a contact email address, we cannot notify you of changes directly. However, for significant updates, we will post a "Privacy Policy Updated" notification as a banner on our home page (www.raysource.co.uk). Your continued use of our Services after any changes are posted constitutes your acknowledgement and acceptance of the updated policy.
