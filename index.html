Zero-Knowledge Suite

Our tools run on Cloudflare Workers to ensure speed and security.
We use client-side encryption before it even hits Cloudflare's servers.

How BurnerMail works:

  1. We give you a temporary, completely random email address (e.g. dge-13532@temp.raysource.co.uk).
  2. Incoming emails are encrypted even before they are sent to the server, then get decrypted directly within your browser.
  3. After an hour, the email address and its received emails are deleted.

By using BurnerMail you are subjected to BurnerMail Guidelines.

How TempFiles works:

  1. When you upload a file, it is encrypted and then sent to Cloudflare's servers as complete gibberish.
  2. You then send a temporary link to someone so they can access this file, while it is decrypted directly within their browser.
  3. After the time you chose, the link expires and the file's contents (which again, is just gibberish to the server) is deleted.

TempFiles is in testing — we've put this here so you know how it works before we make this tool public.
